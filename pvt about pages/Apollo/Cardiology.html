Cardiology

Centre of Excellence in Cardiology – Complete Heart Care Under One Roof

The Centre of Cardiology at Apollo Multispeciality Hospital, Kolkata, offers comprehensive treatment for heart problems and works round the clock to care for your heart in the best possible way. With eminent cardiac specialists, dedicated cardiac operation theatres, digitalized cath labs and cardiac ICUs, your heart receives prime attention.

In any cardiac emergency, time is of vital essence. Thus we start treatment much before you reach hospital. Once we receive a call on our round the clock emergency line 1066, our hospital-on-wheels reaches your doorsteps equipped with doctors, paramedics, ventilators, monitors and life saving medicines. The moment patients arrive in the hospital, our Emergency Unit takes over the care. Medical management, immediate Angioplasty, Surgery – we do whatever is best for you, no matter whatever time the clock may show because for us every hour is a ‘Heart Hour’.
