About :-

Ruby General Hospital is the first NRI hospital in Eastern India. It is ISO 9001 : 2015 certified and a NABH accredited tertiary care multispeciality hospital. Ruby has also been conferred with the prestigious ‘Healthcare Achievers Award’, in the category of Best Multispeciality Hospital, Kolkata. Ruby General Hospital has a very well experienced and accomplished team of medical practitioners, technicians, nurses and other administrative staff. Ruby General Hospital has more than 200 renowned Sr. Consultants of different specialties and around 50 In-house Doctors who are always available. Our Out Patient Services in 42 multi -specialties are available from 8 am to 8 pm (Monday - Saturday) and 8 am - 2 pm (on Sundays) The 316 bedded Hospital has 6 Operation Theatres, Cath Lab, CTVS ICU, ITU, ICCU, NICU, Day Care Services, Radiology Services, Emergency Department, Pathology Services, round the clock ambulance, pharmacy and other diagnostic services. In a span of around 26 years, the hospital has performed a variety of major surgeries & Cath Lab procedures, Cardio Thoracic Surgeries, Cancer Surgeries and other complicated surgeries / procedures with a very high success rate. Every day around 350 to 400 patients avail of our Out Patient Services. The Hospital is equipped with the latest generation equipment and technology. 24 hours Emergency, Trauma Care, Ambulance, Pathology, Pharmacy, Dialysis and Radiology services are available to all. All types of Pathology, Hematology, Biochemistry and a number of other Diagnostics / Investigations are done here. The hospital is also equipped with MRI facilities, Mammography, 128 slice CT Scan, MRCP, MR Angio, all sorts of CT Scans, CT Angio including Cardiac CT are being done here. Around 75 beds have been exclusively dedicated for providing emergency and critical care management of patients. Maximum number of Trauma cases are treated here with a high success rate. There are around 42 multi-specialty departments under which treatment is available, like Cardiology,Pulmonology, Cardiac Surgery, Dental, Endocrinology, ENT, Gastroenterology, General Surgery, Gynaecology and Obstetrics, Haematology, Medicine, Nephrology, Neurology, Neuro Surgery, Orthopaedics, Ophthalmology, Clinical Oncology, Surgical Oncology, Radiation Oncology, Oral & Maxilofacial Surgery, Psychiatry, Pain Management, Neonatal Care and Paediatrics, Paediatric Surgery, Rheumatology, Dermatology, General Medicine, Urology, Uro Gynaecology and others. Ruby Cancer Centre, a unit of Ruby General Hospital is the first NABH accredited Cancer Centre in Kolkata. It has a world class Radiation Oncology service. It also has a comprehensive Cancer Care facility with surgical, medical and radiation oncology services. We have state of the art linear accelerator Clinac iX with FFF (Flattening filter free) technology, the first of its kind in India. We are capable of doing all types of cancer radiotherapy treatment starting from the basic conventional technique to more sophisticated Intensity modulated radiotherapy (IMRT), Image guided radiotherapy (IGRT), Rapid Arc (VMAT) and stereotactic body radiotherapy (SBRT) techniques in a single machine. Besides we have cone beam CT scanner (CBCT) attached to our machine which helps us in taking daily CT scans of our patients prior to the radiation treatment. We also have the latest version of the radiotherapy planning software which helps us to guide the radiation dose to tumor only and sparing the adjacent normal tissues thereby achieving control of the tumor with minimal side effects. We also have a dedicated 24 channel brachytherapy set up (Gammamed Plus) with high dose rate (HDR) radioisotope source. It is also very helpful for treating some of the malignancies in females.

As the whole world grapples with coronavirus, Ruby General Hospital Ltd, has come up to join the rest to fight the virus and eradicate it wholly. It has created additional facilities to accommodate the novel Coronavirus suspects. The doctors and medical staff are strictly abiding by the directions laid down by the Indian Council of Medical Research (ICMR) for diagnosing and treating the COVID-19 patients. It is being touted as one of the best Coronavirus testing centres in India owing to its prompt service delivery. It has procured sufficient number of N95 masks and other essential equipment. It has also taken stern measures so that no one other than the doctors and nurses can enter the isolation ward.
