Cardiac Sciences

Medica offers a wide range of diagnostics as well as therapeutic services for patients with cardiovascular diseases. Cardiac Sciences at Medica’s combines both Cardiology and Cardiac Surgery. Through our collective approach of ensuring the patient’s complete recovery, this division of expertise at our hospital comprises the best cardiac experts from all across the country who have successfully treated numerous cardiac patients. Along with state-of-the-art advanced technology, we have treated complex cases and minimized any risks to the life of the patient. Our team of renowned cardiologists and surgeons diagnose and treat over hundreds of adults and children every year. We are also giving our best to provide treatment for highly complicated cases. Our heart specialists are trained to treat all types of congenital heart and age-induced heart disease in both adults and children. Coronary artery disease, hypertrophic cardiomyopathy, heart rhythm disorders, and heart valve diseases make up for most of the cases that we treat round the year.

Cardiac surgery is usually done on patients with emergency heart issues. It is the only option when no other treatment options are available for a heart condition. Therefore, it requires somebody with the ability to perform life-saving cardiac surgeries with minimum risks. Our surgeons have the skills to perform traditional open-heart surgeries such as coronary artery bypass surgery, angioplasty, valve repair, and other procedures expertly. Our interventional cardiologists, on the other hand, unlike regular cardiologists, are trained to administer particular catheter-based treatments for certain forms of heart disease.

Medica routinely performs complex interventional procedures like:

  • Mitral-Valvuloplasty
  • Permanent Pacemaker Implantation
  • Cardiac Resynchronization Procedures
  • AICD (Implantable Cardioverter-Defibrillator) Implantation

Our doctors are also familiar with procedures for treating congenital ailments such as:

  • Coarctation of Aorta
  • Stenting
  • PDA Closure

Complex Heart Conditions Treated at Medica
