Cardiology is a medical discipline related to the examination and treatment of the blood vessels and the heart. The practitioner dealing with such ailments and the required treatments is known as a cardiologist. This domain falls under internal medicine. It is imperative to know that a cardiologist is different from a cardiac surgeon. A cardiologist specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of the diseases in the cardiovascular system. The cardiologist has a range of tests at his / her disposal like angioplasty, pacemaker insertion, and cardiac catheterizations.
